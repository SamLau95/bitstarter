If you've taken public transportation before, you've probably had to reference the schedules to make sure you get to the station in time to catch your ride. You've also probably noticed that public transportation almost never follows these schedules - a bus could arrive early, making you miss an appointment; a train could arrive too late, making you late to work. There must be a way to definitively know when your public transportation is coming.

Introducing Where's My Bus?, the app that will solve your public transportation frustrations. You can check the precise location of your bus/train through other riders and plan your trip accordingly. Yours and other riders' trips will be recorded and can be referenced to see how late/early your bus/train will be at your stop. Where's My Bus? can even tell you which companies are more reliable than others when it comes to getting you to where you need to be on time. With Where's My Bus, you will never have to spend time at a stop wondering if you missed your bus, saving you time and worry by giving you hard data about your next ride.

FAQ

What can run this app?
Any iPhone, Android, or device with connection to the Internet.
Is the app only used for buses?
No; the app works for any mode of public transportation.
